South Asian country India is officially referred to as the Republic of India (Hindi: Bhrat Gaarjya)[25]. It is currently the seventh-largest country in terms of area and is anticipated to overtake China in 2023 as the world's most populous democracy. Its land borders with Pakistan are to the west, China, Nepal, and Bhutan are to the north, Bangladesh and Myanmar are to the east, and the Indian Ocean, Arabian Sea, and Bay of Bengal are to the south and southwest, respectively. On its Andaman and Nicobar Islands, which are situated in the Indian Ocean close to Sri Lanka and the Maldives, India shares a maritime boundary with Thailand, Burma, and Indonesia.
